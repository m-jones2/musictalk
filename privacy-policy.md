Privacy Policy for SoundZone
Last updated: April 20, 2026

1. Introduction
SoundZone ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your 
privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and 
handle your information when you use the SoundZone mobile 
application.

2. Information We Collect

Anonymous User ID
SoundZone generates a random anonymous identifier for your device 
when you first open the app. This ID is generated locally and is 
not linked to your name, email, or any other personal information.

Display Name
You may optionally enter a display name that is visible to others 
in your voice rooms. This name is stored locally on your device 
and shared with other participants only while you are in an active 
room.

Push Notification Token
With your permission, SoundZone collects your device's push 
notification token to deliver room invitations from other users. 
This token is stored on our server and associated only with your 
anonymous user ID.

Room Participation Data
When you are in a voice room, your anonymous user ID, display name, 
and room code are temporarily stored on our server to enable voice 
chat functionality. This data is automatically deleted when you 
leave the room.

Friends and Contacts
Your friends list and recent contacts are stored entirely on your 
device. This information is never uploaded to our servers.

3. How We Use Your Information
We use the information we collect solely to:
- Enable voice chat functionality between users
- Deliver push notifications for room invitations
- Show your online status to friends who have your user ID

4. Data Sharing
We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with 
third parties. Voice audio is transmitted peer-to-peer via LiveKit, 
a third-party real-time communication platform. Please review 
LiveKit's privacy policy at livekit.io for information about how 
they handle audio data.

5. Data Retention
Room participation data is deleted automatically when you leave a 
room. Push notification tokens are retained until you uninstall 
the app or request deletion. No other data is stored on our servers.

6. Your Rights
You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting 
us. Since your user ID is anonymous, uninstalling the app 
effectively removes all personally identifiable information from 
your device.

7. Children's Privacy
SoundZone is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do 
not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify 
you of any changes by updating the date at the top of this policy.
